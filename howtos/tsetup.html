Expanding your partition

Sign in with the username terraos and pasword terraos.

Once logged in, you have a few options:

# Expand my unnoficial way (reccomended)

# Expand the funny way (reccomended)

# Stop using Terra OS (reccomended)

My way

The way I like to expand is pretty simple:

I boot into a linux install, and open my disk manager.

Then I just select my partition and resize it to max capacity

The funny way

In Terra OS, open your file explorer and go to /, search firefox and wait.

Once you see a folder like /firefox/, find its path.

Open a terminal. Then run the simple command: sudo rm -r (path to firefox)

Now you should have enough room to update and install my favorite disk manager

All you gotta do is run "sudo pacman -Syu gnome-disk-utility"

After installation with (hopefully) no errors, go to your application launcher and search "disks"

Launch you application and find you usb, the find the partition directly left of the free space, right click and select resize,

Then make it fill your desired amount of space
